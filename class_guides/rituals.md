# Rituals (EQL reference)

Distilled from eqlwiki.com Rituals (2026-07-21).

- Rituals let you cast select utility spells WITHOUT that class in your
  active loadout: portal/gate spells (self AND group), Bind Affinity,
  Shrink, druid Ring/Circle lines, and Evacuate spells.
- Unlock is automatic and permanent: when any class of yours reaches
  the spell`s scribe level and scribes it (e.g. Gate at cleric 5), the
  ritual version exists for the character across ALL loadouts forever.
- Casting rules: out of combat in a RESTING state only; flat 20-second
  cast regardless of the original spell; consumes ALL remaining mana on
  completion; some rituals need reagents (Fuligan Soulstone for the
  Plane of Hate port).
- Practical strategy: level a Druid (best port map) or Wizard to ~25
  once — every future loadout keeps full port access. This is also WHY
  the companion never slots travel spells into loadouts: in EQL they
  are rituals, not spell gems.
- The Gate ritual shows your bind location in the UI (7/14 patch).
