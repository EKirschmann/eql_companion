# Races (EQL reference)

Distilled from eqlwiki.com Character_Races (2026-07-21). Race fixes the
PRIMARY class options only (alternate classes are unrestricted);
racials are free abilities you never pay AAs for.

## Standouts for build planning
- Ogre: Frontal Stun Immunity (replicating it costs non-ogre tanks 50+
  AAs via Stalwart Endurance) + Slam. Primaries: BST BER SHD SHM WAR.
  Evil faction. Unlocking Ogre later is notably hard (meta).
- Iksar: enhanced HP regen + scaling AC (+15 to +35) + Forage + swim
  100; -10 cold / +5 fire resist. Primaries: BST MNK NEC SHD SHM WAR.
  "Hated by every race" — expect faction grinds everywhere.
- Troll: enhanced HP regen + Slam; -20 fire resist. Primaries: BST BER
  SHD SHM WAR. Evil.
- Barbarian: Slam, +10 cold resist. Primaries: BST BER ROG SHM WAR.
- Halfling: Hide + Sneak (50), infravision, +5 disease/poison.
  Primaries: CLR DRU PAL RNG ROG WAR.
- Dark Elf: Hide + Ultravision. Primaries: CLR ENC MAG NEC ROG SHD WAR
  WIZ.
- Wood Elf: Forage + Hide. Primaries: BRD DRU RNG ROG WAR.
- Human: no racials; the widest primary list (13 classes).
- Dwarf: infravision, +5 magic/poison. Primaries: BER CLR PAL ROG WAR.
- High Elf: CLR ENC MAG PAL WIZ. Erudite: +5 MR / -5 DR; CLR ENC MAG
  NEC PAL SHD WIZ. Gnome: Tinkering, infravision; 8 primaries.
  Half Elf: BRD DRU PAL RNG ROG WAR. Froglok: enhanced swimming; 9
  primaries incl. MNK (starts Lake Rathe). Kerran: wiki page still
  incomplete.
- Food: Barbarian/Halfling/Ogre/Troll consume 1 unit per 30 min vs 1
  per hour for everyone else.
- Race Unlock Tokens (achievement-earned) can change race later;
  Drakkin is not an option (7/14 patch).
