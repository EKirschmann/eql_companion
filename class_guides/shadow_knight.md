# Shadow Knight (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: unholy plate off-tank/DPS — melee + necromancy, self-healing
  through damage. Meta ranks Paladin above SK generally.
- Harm Touch: innate nuke to ~751, 20-min cooldown, 10 ranks (meta:
  Berserker pairing roughly halves the cooldown).
- Lifetaps are the signature sustain (Lifetap/Lifespike/Lifedraw/
  Spirit Tap/Life Leech/Drain Spirit) — damage that heals YOU with no
  healer needed. NOTE: your own taps log no heal line; the companion
  synthesizes them.
- Feign Death L24; skeleton pet line from L7 (Leering Corpse ->
  Cackling Bones L58); fear + snare crowd control.
- Stances: five melee trade-off stances; invocations Recovery,
  Inversion, Over Channel.
- Watch (necro guide): SK+NEC "Super SK" stacks dual lifetap procs
  (Vampiric Embrace + Scream of Death L39 AoE lifetap proc + exaltation
  lifetap weapons).
