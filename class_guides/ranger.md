# Ranger (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: offense-leaning hybrid (Warrior+Druid) — "light tank with some
  of the best aggro-inducing abilities"; not for sustained tanking
  (lower defensive caps than WAR/PAL).
- Archery: DOUBLE damage vs standing, non-rooted targets; Ranged
  Stance removes minimum range, +25% accuracy, enables double/triple
  bow attacks; bow crits by default. A no-mana damage mode.
- Highest tracking skill in the game (finds mobs AND group corpses
  outdoors). Highest offensive skill caps of any hybrid; Strikethrough
  prevents ripostes.
- Utility: snare/root from L15, Spirit of Wolf L28, Wolf Form L36 (55%
  run, +30 ATK), heals/damage shields/AC buffs; NO bind/gate (use
  rituals); no hate-shedding until Jolt at L50.
- Stances: Balanced/Offensive/Evasive/Ranged; invocations Recovery,
  Inversion, Overchannel, Spellblade, Divine.
- Trio: snare-anchor + tracker + no-mana ranged DPS; mana pool counts
  WIS (see general pool rule).
