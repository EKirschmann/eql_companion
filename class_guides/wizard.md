# Wizard (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: pure burst caster — "massive damage as rapidly as possible";
  ports for the group. Contributes little besides damage + travel (root
  is the only control). Cloth armor.
- Landmarks: quad-kiting AoEs (Column of Frost, Pillar of Fire, Circle
  of Force, L15-31), Conflagration ~900 damage and Supernova ~854 AoE
  by L35-50, root-nuke combos (Entombment of Ice), gates + group
  translocations + planar access.
- Stances/invocations: Channeler (40% DR to mana/end), Recovery, Over
  Channel (-150 resist adjust), Empowering (+20% damage for +20% cost).
- Mana-bound: shines on burst windows and bad groups, starves in
  fast-pull optimized groups.
- Trio: the ritual answer to travel (level to 25 once for ports — see
  general guide); DoT-resistant bosses favor wizard burst.
