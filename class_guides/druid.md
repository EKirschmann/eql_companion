# Druid (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: nature priest — heals, ports, damage shields, quad-kiting;
  solos and earns well ("money via transports, item farming, quad
  killing giants"). Meta ranks DRU alongside SHM behind Cleric.
- Ports: the widest network in Norrath (Ring/Circle/Zephyr lines, 15+
  destinations; group evacs). Ritual note: level a Druid to ~25 ONCE
  and its ports work from any loadout (see general guide).
- Heals: Minor -> Greater Healing -> Healing Water plus HoT line
  (Budding/Sprouting/Flowering/Blooming — final Bloom ~300);
  Regeneration L34.
- Damage shields: Thistles/Barbs/Brambles/Spikes on others,
  Thistlecoat->Bramblecoat self — passive damage on any tank.
- Stances/invocations: Channeler, Recovery, Divine (mana spent heals
  lowest-HP member), Empowering.
- Caveats: several key spells are OUTDOOR-only; animal charm to ~L43;
  fire/cold nukes + swarm DoTs; snare/root control.
