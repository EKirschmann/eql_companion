# Paladin (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: defensive plate tank with real healing — meta calls Paladin the
  best non-priest healer and ranks it above SK.
- Lay on Hands: 15-min instant heal scaling to ~6251; landing Smite
  cuts its cooldown by 60s (meta: Berserker pairing roughly halves it).
  Can target PETS (7/14 patch: divine invocation targets self/group
  pets at lower priority) — clutch for pet builds.
- Stances: Balanced, Offensive, Defensive (50% melee/20% magic DR),
  Mage Hunter (50% spell / 20% physical DR).
- Invocations: Recovery, Divine (mana spent heals lowest-HP member),
  Spellblade (spell slots become melee procs).
- Undead specialist: Ward/Expulse/Dismiss Undead, undead-invis, Slay
  Undead AA (445-1250% damage procs).
- Landmarks: stuns from L7 (Cease) and L28, Wave of Life group heal
  L39, Divine Aura 18s invulnerability L48, Retribution L50.
