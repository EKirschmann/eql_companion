# Berserker (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: savage two-hander melee DPS — pure damage, no support tools.
- Stances (six): Balanced, Offensive (+100%), Striker (3-5x ability),
  Mage Hunter (50% spell resistance), Ranged (throwing), and the
  signature Berserker Stance — DOUBLED attack speed at 8.3% self-damage
  (meta: rated the strongest stance in the game).
- Skills: Frenzy (rapid multi-strike signature), Double Attack L15,
  Triple Attack L46; new disciplines Tendon Slice (L14 snare) and
  Skull Bash (L20 stun) added 7/14.
- AAs: Blood Rune (absorb shield on crits), Innate Power Strike,
  Unbound Fury (+2% crit/rank).
- Trio synergy (meta): pairing BER with PAL/SK roughly halves Lay on
  Hands / Harm Touch cooldowns and speeds reave/smite extra attacks.
  Endurance management gates sustained peak output.
