# Cleric (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: the strongest healer in the game ("no one can match clerical
  healing"), plate + blunt. Stated: mostly group-dependent.
- Stances/invocations: Channeler (40% DR, absorbed damage splits to
  mana/end), Recovery (2x mana regen, -5% costs), Divine (heals scale
  with mana spent; extra WIS classes add 33%-value heals), Over Channel
  (spell penetration for endurance).
- Toolkit: top single-target + group heals (Superior Healing 600 at
  L30, Word of Health), long AC/HP buffs (Center/Spirit Armor/Valor,
  27-54 min), resurrections restoring 10-50% XP, undead nukes/fear
  (Expulse/Dismiss Undead), lull/calm/root crowd control.
- No self-invisibility (undead-only invis at L11).
- Trio: the survival anchor; meta ranks CLR first among healers.
