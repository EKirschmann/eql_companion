# Magician (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: master summoner — the strongest classic pet class plus conjured
  gear. Pet-dependent, little crowd control or healing.
- Pets: four elements (Water/Fire/Air/Earth) scaling by tier; Monster
  Summoning from L30. Burnout pet haste L11. Remember: summon-spell
  TIER adds pet levels (+6% HP, +1 damage each; general guide).
- Damage shields: Shield of Fire L7 -> Inferno Shield L28 — group-cast,
  fire-based, described as unresistable; strong passive damage layer.
- Stances/invocations: Channeler, Recovery, Overchannel (-150 resist
  adjust), Empowering (+20% spell damage).
- Utility: summoned no-rent food/weapons/armor/bags (10-slot at L51),
  Waterstone, levitation ring; Malaise resist debuff L22; Bind Affinity
  L12.
- Trio: pet tank + DS + resist debuffs; excellent early solo speed
  (pets are exceptionally good under ~12 — meta).
