# Monk (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: unarmed/blunt melee specialist — sustained DPS with
  tank-adjacent defensive disciplines; solos well. Gear-light (fists
  are good), limited armor.
- Stances: Balanced, Offensive (+100%), Striker (3-5x ability damage),
  Evasive (95% evade at 2 end/point), Ranged (throwing).
- Disciplines: Stonestance (60% melee DR, L51), Voiddance (full melee
  evasion, L54), Innerflame (+100% damage, L56).
- Signature abilities: Feign Death at L17 (pull control / escapes),
  Mend self-heal, Dragon Punch/Tail Rake (L25), Flying Kick (L30),
  early Sneak + Safe Fall mobility.
- Trio: steady melee damage + FD pulling; meta note: skill overlap
  makes WAR+MNK a weak pairing; MNK+ENC mez-then-FD is a clean escape.
