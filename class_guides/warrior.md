# Warrior (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: the premier tank — highest HP pool, plate, all melee weapon
  types, best mitigation and threat tools. Stated weakness: "fare poorly
  when adventuring alone"; zero mana, little vs magic-immune foes.
- Stances: Balanced (10% DR, 2x end regen), Offensive (+100% damage,
  +25% hit), Striker (3-5x ability damage), Defensive (50% melee / 20%
  magic damage reduction) — the tanking anchor.
- Berserker Frenzy passive: below 40% HP, crits convert to high-damage
  stuns until back to 45%.
- Aggro toolkit: Heroic Leap (L12, leap + 8-target hate), Area Taunt +
  War Cry fear-immunity group buff (L25).
- Trio: unmatched mitigation/threat lets fragile classes work; pairs
  with any healer. Meta note: avoid stacking with Monk (skill overlap).
