# Necromancer (EQL)

Source: distilled from "Multiclass Guide — How It Works & The Best
Necromancer Combos (2026)" by Haitsmelol (necrotalk.com, May 2026 closed
beta; shared on r/EQLegends) plus the beta FAQ thread. Meta moves fast —
re-verify after patches.

## What Necromancer brings to ANY trio
- Feign Death: premier solo utility — reach deep camps, shed aggro,
  recover bad pulls. EQL deaths cost no XP and respawn in-zone, so
  FD-rushing camps is low-risk.
- Vampiric Embrace: passive melee lifesteal proc scaling Level + 12
  (72/proc at 60) — notably stronger for NEC than the SK version of the
  same spell. Free sustain for melee trios.
- DoT stack: among the most mana-efficient damage in the game — ideal on
  long fights and raid bosses, and lets you sit in defensive stances.
- Pet: permanent gear slots persist across summons — keep it geared even
  in hybrid builds.
- Invocations: Recovery, Inversion, Overchannel, Empowering — brings
  caster invocations to melee trios that would otherwise have none.

## Proven combos
- WAR/NEC/BRD — the current powerhouse: Warrior Defensive Stance (50%
  melee damage reduction) + Necro sustain (Vampiric Embrace, DoTs, FD) +
  Bard Symphonic Aura AA (free at level 1: auto-pulses up to 5 zero-mana
  non-targeted songs — Selo`s, haste, melee buffs, regen — while /melody
  runs more; ~9 concurrent songs). Race: Ogre (free frontal stun
  immunity; non-ogres pay 50+ AAs for Stalwart Endurance).
- WAR/NEC/DRU — the traveler: durable leveling build with Druid ports,
  damage shield, and conventional heals. Once Druid is leveled, RITUALS
  keep the ports usable from any loadout — a deliberate stepping stone;
  swap Druid out later without losing travel.
- MNK/NEC/ROG — deep-camp specialist: FD-rush + Rogue Sneak (strongest
  invis in the game; only top raid bosses see through it) to reach camps
  others can`t — Frenzied Ghoul / Ghoul Assassin (Guise) in Lower Guk,
  Rubicite in Cazic-Thule. Monk Striker Stance (5x special multiplier) +
  Rogue Backstab (best backstab weapon works from Primary, Secondary, OR
  Any slot) + DoTs for the kill.
- Watch: "Super SK" (SK+NEC dual lifetap procs — Vampiric Embrace +
  Scream of Death, the lv39 AoE lifetap proc — plus an exaltation
  lifetap weapon proc); multi-pet trios (NEC/MAG/BST) are capped at ONE
  active pet for now — devs say pet classes need attention.

## Notes
- Melee-forward NEC builds outperform pure-caster trios for solo sustain
  in the current meta.
- Beastlord pairing is weak right now — "stick with a shaman" (beta FAQ).
- Necro lich-style trade-life-for-mana lines do NOT stack with Clarity.
