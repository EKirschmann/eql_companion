# General EQL mechanics & meta (all trios)

Sources: eqltools.com combat/attributes pages (client-mined + dev
quotes), necrotalk.com multiclass guide (May 2026), u/token_friend beta
FAQ (r/EQLegends 2026-07-14), official patch notes 7/7 + 7/14. Open-beta
era — re-verify after patches.

## Multiclass rules
- Trio = primary (race-restricted, LOCKS at level 11) + 2 alternates
  (swappable in any major city). Levels 1-10 ALL classes gain XP; after
  10 only the active loadout. EFFECTIVE level = LOWEST of the three —
  keep the trio level-synced.
- All spells/abilities/stances/invocations of all three classes are
  available (one stance + one invocation active at a time). Spell Gem
  Expansion AA is a must-buy. Rituals: port/bind/gate of any class you
  ever leveled far enough work from ANY loadout — level Druid or Wizard
  to 25 once for ports forever.
- Primary should usually be a tank or healer (most flexible); it is the
  only unswappable slot.

## Pools & stats
- HP/mana/endurance = the TWO highest class pools summed; the third is
  dropped (a third caster adds no mana). Exact pool formula unpublished.
- Soft caps 200 WIS/INT/CHA (hard 255); ~150 naked ceiling reported;
  pet gear stat total caps at 510.

## Combat math (client-mined)
- Four numbers: Accuracy+Offense = displayed ATK; Mitigation+Evasion =
  displayed AC. Defenses check riposte -> parry -> dodge -> block, then
  hit roll, then a 20-slot damage roll: Offense pushes the average roll
  up, Mitigation down; the endpoints never move. STR above 75 adds ~2/3
  Offense per point (STR/DEX raise the chance to hit FOR max damage, not
  the damage itself).
- Skills use the BEST cap among the trio. Mitigation soft-caps per
  class/level; Evasion is uncapped; shield AC BYPASSES the mitigation
  soft cap. Haste cap ~175% at 50 (low-level item-haste cap removed 7/7).

## Meta consensus (beta — changes fast)
- A healer is near-mandatory: CLR > SHM/DRU > PAL > RNG/BST. Paladin >
  SK (Lay on Hands + undead tools; best non-priest healer).
- A SLOWER (ENC, BRD, SHM, or BST) is required for D3+; only exception
  is a heavily defensive WAR+CLR build.
- Warrior is the best tank (avoid WAR+MNK — heavy overlap). Berserker
  paired with PAL/SK roughly halves Lay on Hands / Harm Touch cooldowns
  and speeds their reave/smite extra attacks.
- Stance/invocation swapping is essential; Berserker stance and the
  Inversion invocation rate strongest overall.
- Motes: spend on SPELLS, not gear — the highest spell in a series or
  leveling accelerators (pet summons). Don't over-farm low-level +N
  gear; it's replaced fast from ~42. Fastest AA/mote income: level a
  fresh class 10-20 in D4 (white/yellow/red cons, multi-class and named
  mobs drop the best motes).
- Pets: strong under ~12; summon-spell TIER adds pet level (cap player
  -1; each level +6% HP, +1 base damage). Pet weapon rule: a pet swings
  weapon damage x2 when that beats its natural max hit — DELAY IS
  IRRELEVANT for pets. Pet gear persists across summons and zoning.
- Any slots: Rogue -> best backstab dagger (works from Any); everyone
  else -> shields (shield AC bypasses the soft cap).
- Raid ladder: Yael < Nagafen < Vox < Hate < Fear < Sky; Sky is the
  endgame hub (class unlocks + best gear).
