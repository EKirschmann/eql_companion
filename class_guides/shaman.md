# Shaman (EQL)

Baseline distilled from the eqlwiki.com class page (2026-07-21). Replace/extend with community wisdom as it appears; front-load decision-relevant facts.

- Role: tribal support priest — "the most powerful slowing spells and
  resistance debuffs in the game" plus real heals; meta ranks SHM just
  behind Cleric and counts it as a SLOWER for D3+.
- Slows: Drowsy L5 -> Walking Sleep L13 -> Tagar`s Insects L27 (~50%
  attack-speed cut at the top). Haste: Quickness L26, Alacrity L42
  (+30-40%).
- Stances/invocations: Channeler (40% DR to mana/end), Recovery, Divine
  (mana spent heals lowest-HP member), Over Channel, Empowering.
- Toolkit: stat buff suite (Spirit of Wolf/Bear/Cat/Ox lines, +40 stats
  by L39-43 — no WIS/INT buffs), strong poison/disease DoTs, heals to
  456 at L40 + HoTs, Cannibalize HP->mana (L23), permanent pet at L32,
  animal charm to ~L33.
- Iksar shamans start KoS in many places (faction).
