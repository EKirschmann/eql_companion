# Stances & Invocations (EQL reference)

Distilled from eqlwiki.com Stances_&_Invocations (2026-07-21). One
stance + one invocation active at a time; all granted at level 1; each
has a 6s reuse (separate cooldown tracks); upkeep costs are reduced by
the Strategy skill. KEY: several invocations SCALE WITH TRIO
COMPOSITION — the math changes per build (noted below).

## Stances
- Balanced (all melee/hybrids): 10% DR, +10% hit, 2x end regen — FREE.
- Offensive (BER BRD BST MNK PAL RNG ROG SHD WAR): +100% damage, +25%
  hit; endurance cost equals damage dealt (reduced under 25% end).
- Striker (BER MNK ROG WAR): 3x weapon-skill / 5x non-weapon-skill
  damage, +25% hit.
- Defensive (PAL SHD WAR): 50% melee / 20% magic DR; end cost equals
  the damage reduced.
- Evasive (BRD BST MNK RNG ROG): 95% evade; 2 end per point evaded.
- Mage Hunter (BER PAL SHD): 50% spell / 20% physical DR.
- Ranged (BER MNK RNG ROG): no minimum range, +25% accuracy,
  double/triple ranged attacks.
- Berserker (BER only): 2x attack speed AND skill recharge, +25%
  hit/damage; 8.3% of outgoing damage self-inflicted.
- Channeler (CLR DRU ENC MAG NEC SHM WIZ): 40% DR; half the mitigated
  damage drains mana and endurance.

## Invocations
- Recovery (all caster/hybrid classes): 2x mana regen, -5% costs.
- Inversion (same list): 2/3 of cast time shifts into recovery; 20% of
  mana cost becomes half as much endurance — fast casts, slow recovery.
- Over Channel (same list): -150 resist adjustment, an EXTRA -15 per
  non-hybrid caster class in the trio; 10% of mana cost paid as end.
- Empower (CLR DRU ENC MAG NEC SHM WIZ): +20% spell damage (+10% per
  extra non-hybrid caster) for +20% mana; +10% healing (+5%/caster).
- Arcane Mastery (ENC MAG NEC SHD WIZ): -20% cast/recovery time (+10%
  per additional INT class), -10% spell cost (+5% per INT class).
- Divine (BST CLR DRU PAL RNG SHM): mana spent heals the lowest-HP
  group member (+33% per additional WIS class); since 7/14 it also
  targets self/group pets at 20%/40% lower priority.
- Spellblade (BST PAL RNG SHD): first spell gem becomes a melee proc at
  40% mana + 20% end (only spells with reuse <= cast time).
- Unyielding (BER MNK ROG WAR): 2x in-combat health regen, 25%
  fear/mez/charm resistance — free.
- Inviolable (BRD WIZ): uninterruptible casting at double mana + equal
  endurance.

Playstyle math: a triple-INT-caster trio pushes Arcane Mastery to -40%
cast time; two WIS classes make Divine heal +33% harder per mana; three
non-hybrid casters take Over Channel to -180/-195 resist adjust.
Empowering and OverChannel also apply to DoTs and HoTs (7/7 patch).
